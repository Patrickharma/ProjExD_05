Free Samurai Pixel Art Sprite Sheets

Description

We present you a cool Free Samurai Pixel Art Sprite Sheets. They have cool and high-quality graphics. This pack contains 3 characters (samurai) that can be easily used to create an interesting 2D game project.

There are characters here such as:
  • Samurai in blue armor, and a katana;
  • Samurai with a longbow, arrows and red and white armor;
  • Samurai commander, with a katana and a family banner.

This is a very useful package that will help you create some really fun gameplay. Your players will definitely appreciate the quality and skills of the characters. In addition, in the archive you can find sprite sheets with unique samurai animations. Several types of attacks, dead, hurt, idle, jump, protect, run, walk. These animations will help you create something new. For example, battles with rebels who went against the honor of the emperor. Also, if you wish, you can easily change this set. For example, you can add new banners or elements of the traditional samurai costume, family coats of arms, change the color of the armor, reduce the size, or vice versa, scale it. Use Adobe Photoshop or any other graphics editor. This package is made in pixel art style, which means that all files are stored in PSD and PNG formats.

  • Graphics: 2D, Pixel Art;
  • Quantity: 3 characters and 10 sprite sheets;
  • Format: PSD, PNG.
